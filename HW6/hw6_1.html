About San Francisco

San Francisco is a uniquely city, whose scenic attractions include the largest park in the country, Golden Gate Park. It's notoriously steep streets, traversed by the famous cable cars, are home to a remarkably diverse ethnic population. Today the Bay Area is also famous for its concentration of high-technology firms.
